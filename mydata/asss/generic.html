Overview:

The combination of AR and 5G can significantly reduce the cost of AR glasses. All image clips and combinations can be processed through the cloud and then transmitted to each user to get the image they want. For example, previous AR devices were too bulky and required the necessary equipment to be installed on the glasses. However, with 5G transmission speeds, we can boldly take these devices apart and focus on one place to process image information. There are two advantages here: first, it is convenient. Every AR glasses can be moved to any position without any weight problems. Second, reducing costs allows more users to experience things they haven't experienced before. For example, virtual characters can be seen standing in front of them in a combination of virtual and reality.
